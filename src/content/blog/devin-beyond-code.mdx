## The blind spot

- Devin's marketing (rightly) leads with code: migrations, PR review, refactors.
- But "you can offload the manual work; you can't offload critical thinking" cuts both ways.

## Five non-coding workflows worth a curriculum module

1. Web research + synthesis (the DANA / data-analyst angle).
2. Living documentation + system diagrams (DeepWiki).
3. Design-system maintenance and enforcement.
4. Recurring chores: release notes, QA, triage.
5. GTM / ops automation (the work I do at Keysight).

## Why this matters for community

- It widens the funnel beyond senior engineers — PMs, analysts, founders.
- Non-coders becoming power users is the highest-leverage growth story.

## A teachable framework

- The "orchestrator mindset": describe outcomes, review trajectories, build playbooks.
