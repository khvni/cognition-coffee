## What Cursor got right

- Cafe Cursor: take over a cafe, bring coffee + credits, meet local builders.
- Centralized on Luma; social-proof tweet wall; 300+ ambassadors.
- Warm, human, low-friction — the opposite of a sterile launch page.

## Where pop-ups hit a ceiling

- One-off energy doesn't compound without local ownership.
- The MTC lesson: chapters > events. Recurring local leadership is the unlock.

## The Cafe Cognition kit (meetup-in-a-box)

- Run-of-show, slide deck, live-demo script, swag templates, photo checklist.
- A host leaderboard that turns great organizers into a flywheel.

## The three-tier chapter model (from MTC)

- National → Regional → Local. Who owns what, and why it scales.
- How 1 Berkeley club became 30+ chapters in 18 months.

## Devin Days

- Quarterly flagship hack days in SF, NYC, Austin, London, Bangalore.

## Metrics that matter

- Active chapters, repeat hosts, events/month, builds shipped — not vanity counts.
